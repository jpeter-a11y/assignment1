About GITAM

GITAM (Deemed to be University) Vizag , ESTD 1980 is one of the leading universities in AP It offers various courses with high quality education , the campus is located in Rushikonda and has a good overall environment. The campus atmosphere is friendly and the night life in GITAM Vizag is almost unreal , The students enjoy the university inside and out with great academic and social life
